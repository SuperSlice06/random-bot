Information Commands

avatar

Gets the avatar of a user.

botinfo

Sends detailed info about the bot.

calc

Calculates a math equation

membercount

Member count for server

roleinfo

Get the info of a role.

serverinfo

Shows info about a server.

userinfo

Get information about a user.


Moderation Commands

addrole

Adds a role to a mentioned user.

removerole

Removes a role to a mentioned user.

ban

Bans a mentioned user.

unban

Unbans a mentioned user.

clear

Deletes a specified number of messages in a text channel.

kick

Kicks a mentioned user.

lock

Locks a channel.

unlock

Unlocks a channel.

mute

Mutes a mentioned user.

unmute

Unmutes a mentioned user.

slowmode

Enable/disable slowmode.

invite

Invite Random Bot to your server.
